mcel is the leading mobile network operator in Mozambique, with about 3.5 million active subscribers where 98% are prepaid. It commands 75% of market share. Its network is based on the GSM standard and is operating on both 2.5/2.75G (almost over the whole country) and 3G layers in the 5 main cities namely Maputo, Beira, Tete, Nampula and Nacal. An aggressive coverage expansion on both 2G and 3G is ongoing.


As part of mcel's CRM strategy, the company seeks to rewards its loyal customers by upgrading them from prepaid package to either NTDUO or postpaid, depending on the customer's income, usage history and preferences. This web portal therefore seeks to enhance mcel's relationship with its prepaid customers by offering a platform where they can conduct business with mcel online and simplify the application process to the convenience of the customer. All mcel customers can register onine, make applications and track the progress of their application online and only have to visit mcel shops to deliver documents or collect SIM card and gadgets.
